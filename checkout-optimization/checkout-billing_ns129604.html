The Nutrisystem Program is a portion-controlled, reduced-calorie comprehensive weight loss program. You are urged and advised to seek the advice of your health care provider or a physician before beginning the Nutrisystem Program, or any other weight loss program, as changing your diet or exercise, or losing weight, may affect some medical conditions and medications.
